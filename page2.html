⤛CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE⤜
Maria Clara
  • The fiance of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Later found out in the story that she is the biological daughter of Padre Damaso and not Kapitan Tiago
  • In the story, she represents the ideal Filino woman in Philippine society.
    		•
    Kapitan Tiago
  • Rich, snobby and influencial person in the town of San Diego.
  • Although he is pure Filipino, he trully sees himself as a Spaniard.
  • Was married to Pia Alba who died due to child birth.
    		•
    Padre Damaso
  • Main antagonist of the story.
  • Best known for his cruelty as a prince and use of harsh words.
